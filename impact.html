Spotlight

Dynamic Pricing & Cohort-Based Incentive Engine

Replaced static FX and fee models with a cohort-aware pricing system across 155+ corridors at BOTIM Ultra.

  • Segmented users - new, repeat, high-value, drop-off, corridor - using transaction behavior, success rates, and volume.
  • Targeted offers: fee waivers, cashback, next-transaction-free, preferential FX.
  • Corridor-level pricing logic aligned to partner cost (payout fees) and market competitiveness.
  • A/B testing across cohorts for pricing elasticity and offer effectiveness.
  • ~19% YoY revenue growth, higher AOV, lower incentive spend on low-impact cohorts.

Advisory

Advisory for early-stage fintech - from 0 to 1, and 1 to scale.

I work with founders building payments, remittance, wallet, and digital banking products. Hands-on with regulators, partners, and the messy first 12 months of a fintech roadmap.

01 · Product Foundations

From idea to MVP that regulators accept.

Scoping, vendor selection, partner due-diligence (banks, BIN sponsors, payout networks), and a launch-ready roadmap that won't collapse on audit day.

02 · Onboarding & KYC

Activation that converts without breaking compliance.

OCR-based KYC, identity stack selection (Alloy, Socure, Sumsub, Onfido), step-reduction, drop-off analytics, and the trade-offs between conversion and risk.

03 · Payments & Remittance

Cross-border that actually moves.

Corridor strategy, payout partner integrations (TerraPay, JinglePay, MoneyGram, Wise), FX & pricing models, and operational monitoring that catches failures before customers do.

04 · Cards & Wallets

Issuance, lifecycle, and the boring middle.

BIN sponsor selection, tokenization, ISO 8583 flows, PCI-DSS scoping, chargebacks, settlement, and reconciliation. Crypto wallet readiness for regulated markets.

05 · Growth & Monetization

Pricing that respects the customer and the P&L.

Cohort-based pricing, dynamic FX, incentive design, A/B framework, and unit-economics modeling at the corridor or segment level.
